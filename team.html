Implementation Methodology

  • Deep context analysis of business operations, constraints, and opportunities

  • Technical feasibility assessment and architecture planning
  • ROI modeling and success metrics definition

Phase 2: Development & Training (8-16 weeks)

  • Data collection, cleaning, and preparation
