AI Risk Categories

Technical Risks

• Model accuracy and performance degradation

• System reliability and availability issues

• Data quality and bias problems

• Security vulnerabilities and cyber threats

Operational Risks

• Process disruption and business continuity

• Human oversight and control mechanisms

• Change management and adoption challenges

• Vendor dependency and technology lock-in

Regulatory & Compliance Risks

• Data privacy and protection violations

• Algorithmic accountability requirements

• Industry-specific regulatory compliance

• Ethical AI standards and guidelines

Strategic & Business Risks

• Competitive disadvantage and market position

• Reputation damage and brand impact

• Financial losses and ROI failures

• Talent and capability gaps

Risk Assessment Process

Identification: Comprehensive risk inventory across all AI initiatives

Analysis: Impact and probability assessment using quantitative methods

Evaluation: Risk prioritization and tolerance threshold determination

Treatment: Mitigation strategies and control implementation

Monitoring: Continuous risk tracking and control effectiveness review

Mitigation Strategies

Preventive Controls: Design safeguards, validation processes, and quality gates

Detective Controls: Monitoring systems, anomaly detection, and performance alerts

Corrective Controls: Incident response procedures and recovery mechanisms

Governance Controls: Oversight committees, approval processes, and audit procedures
