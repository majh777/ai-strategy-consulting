AI Maturity Model Framework

Comprehensive framework for assessing organizational AI maturity and developing advancement roadmaps.

AI Maturity Levels

Level 1: Initial (Ad-hoc)

Limited AI awareness, experimental projects, no formal strategy or governance. Technology-driven initiatives with unclear business value.

Level 2: Developing (Managed)

Formal AI strategy emerging, pilot projects underway, basic data infrastructure. Some governance frameworks and skill development programs.

Level 3: Defined (Standardized)

Established AI processes, successful production deployments, standardized tools and platforms. Dedicated AI teams and centers of excellence.

Level 4: Managed (Measured)

Systematic AI deployment, performance measurement, continuous improvement. Integrated business processes and mature governance frameworks.

Level 5: Optimized (Innovation)

AI-first organization, autonomous systems, continuous innovation. AI embedded in strategic decision-making and competitive advantage.

Assessment Dimensions

Strategy & Governance: Vision, leadership, policies, and decision-making processes

Technology & Infrastructure: Platforms, tools, data architecture, and technical capabilities

People & Skills: Talent, training, culture, and organizational capabilities

Processes & Operations: Workflows, methodologies, and operational excellence

Data & Analytics: Data quality, availability, and analytical sophistication

Advancement Roadmap

Current State Assessment: Comprehensive evaluation across all maturity dimensions

Target State Definition: Strategic objectives and desired maturity level

Gap Analysis: Identification of specific improvements needed

Implementation Plan: Prioritized initiatives and timeline

Progress Monitoring: Regular assessment and course correction
