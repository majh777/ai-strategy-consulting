Moroccan Port: AI Container Scheduling Increases Throughput 35%

Smart logistics optimization transforms North Africa's gateway to Europe

Port Operations Logistics AI Morocco Maritime Optimization

Client Profile

Port Tanger Med operates Morocco's largest container terminal, handling 5.8 million TEU annually and serving as a critical transshipment hub connecting Africa, Europe, and the Americas. The port employs 3,400 workers and operates 24/7 with 67 ship-to-shore cranes across 4.2 kilometers of quayside.

As North Africa's busiest port and a key component of Morocco's economic strategy, Tanger Med faced mounting pressure to increase capacity without major infrastructure expansion while maintaining service quality for global shipping lines.

The Challenge

Capacity Constraints and Congestion

Port capacity had reached 94% utilization during peak seasons, creating ship waiting times averaging 18 hours and customer complaints about delayed departures. Traditional manual scheduling relied on experienced port planners but couldn't optimize the complex interplay of ship arrivals, crane availability, storage allocation, and truck/rail connections.

Complex Multi-Modal Coordination

Efficient port operations require seamless coordination across multiple systems:

  • Ship berthing schedules balancing vessel priorities and berth utilization
  • Crane assignment optimization considering container types and destinations
  • Yard storage management for container stacking and retrieval efficiency
  • Ground transportation coordination for truck and rail container pickups
  • Weather contingency planning for operations during Atlantic storms

Competitive Pressure

Competing Mediterranean ports offered faster turnaround times, threatening Tanger Med's position as the preferred transshipment hub. Shipping lines increasingly chose ports based on operational efficiency metrics, making speed and reliability critical competitive differentiators.

AI Solution Architecture

Integrated Port Operations Intelligence

Ashdown AI deployed a comprehensive port optimization platform combining predictive analytics, real-time scheduling, and resource allocation:

Predictive Arrival Management: Machine learning algorithms analyze vessel tracking data, weather conditions, and port traffic to predict ship arrivals with 96% accuracy within 2-hour windows, enabling proactive berth and resource preparation.

Dynamic Crane Optimization: The AI system assigns crane resources in real-time based on ship cargo profiles, discharge/loading sequences, and equipment maintenance schedules to minimize vessel turnaround times while maximizing crane utilization.

Intelligent Yard Management: Algorithms optimize container stacking patterns and locations to minimize retrieval time for onward transportation, considering destination patterns, dwell times, and container characteristics.

Multi-Modal Integration: The platform coordinates ship operations with truck arrivals, rail schedules, and customs processing to eliminate bottlenecks and ensure smooth cargo flow through the entire port ecosystem.

Implementation Process

Phase 1: Data Integration (Months 1-3)

We integrated real-time data feeds from ship tracking systems, crane monitoring equipment, yard management systems, and weather services. Our team established APIs connecting with customs systems, shipping line schedules, and ground transportation providers to create a comprehensive operational data platform.

Phase 2: AI Model Development (Months 4-6)

Using 5 years of historical port operations data, we trained machine learning models for demand prediction, resource optimization, and schedule coordination. The AI system learned patterns in seasonal cargo flows, shipping line preferences, and operational constraints specific to Tanger Med's infrastructure.

Phase 3: Pilot Implementation (Months 7-8)

We deployed the system for one container terminal, optimizing operations for 23% of total port capacity while maintaining existing procedures for other terminals. The pilot demonstrated significant improvements in vessel turnaround times and crane productivity without disrupting ongoing operations.

Phase 4: Port-Wide Deployment (Months 9-12)

Following pilot success, we expanded the platform across all four container terminals, training 156 port operators and integrating AI recommendations into existing control room procedures and planning workflows.

Quantified Business Impact

Dramatic Throughput Enhancement

Container throughput increased from 5.8 million to 7.8 million TEU annually—a 35% improvement achieved without infrastructure expansion. The AI optimization enabled the port to handle additional capacity equivalent to building a new terminal worth €340 million.

Operational Efficiency Revolution

Ship waiting times decreased from 18 hours to 9.5 hours average, while crane productivity increased 29% through optimized scheduling and reduced idle time. Container retrieval times improved by 52%, enabling faster truck and rail turnaround.

Revenue and Competitive Advantage

Increased throughput and efficiency improvements generated €89 million in additional annual revenue. Customer satisfaction among shipping lines increased from 7.1/10 to 9.2/10, securing long-term service agreements worth €234 million.

Environmental and Sustainability Benefits

Reduced ship waiting times decreased harbor emissions by 34%, while optimized ground transportation coordination reduced truck idling by 43%. These improvements supported Morocco's environmental commitments while reducing operational costs.

Lessons Learned

Real-Time Adaptability is Critical

Port operations face constant disruptions from weather, equipment failures, and schedule changes. The AI system's ability to rapidly recalculate optimal schedules proved more valuable than static optimization, preventing cascading delays throughout the terminal.

Stakeholder Coordination Multiplies Benefits

The greatest efficiency gains occurred when AI optimization extended beyond port operations to include shipping lines, customs authorities, and ground transportation providers. Coordinated scheduling across all stakeholders eliminated traditional bottlenecks.

Operator Expertise Enhances AI

Experienced port operators provided valuable insights for refining AI recommendations, particularly regarding equipment limitations and safety considerations. Human-AI collaboration proved more effective than fully automated decision making.

Predictive Maintenance Integration

Incorporating equipment maintenance predictions into scheduling algorithms prevented breakdowns during peak operations while optimizing maintenance timing to minimize disruption. This integration proved essential for maintaining consistent performance.

Regional Maritime Hub Strategy

Tanger Med is expanding the AI optimization platform to include its automotive and logistics terminals, creating an integrated smart port ecosystem. The success has attracted interest from other Moroccan ports seeking similar efficiency improvements.

Plans include integration with trans-Saharan logistics corridors and development of predictive analytics for African trade flows, positioning Morocco as the continent's premier gateway for international commerce.
