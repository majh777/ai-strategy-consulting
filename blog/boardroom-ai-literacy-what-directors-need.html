Questions Directors Should Ask

Strategic AI Questions

Business Strategy Alignment:

  • How does our AI strategy support overall business objectives?
  • What competitive advantages will AI create for our organization?
  • How are we measuring AI success beyond technical metrics?
  • What happens if our AI initiatives fail to deliver expected results?

Resource and Capability Assessment:

  • Do we have adequate talent and expertise for AI success?
  • How are we building internal AI capabilities vs. relying on vendors?
  • What data assets do we have that create competitive advantage?
  • How sustainable are our AI investments over the long term?

Risk and Governance Questions

Risk Management:

  • What are our greatest AI-related risks and how are we mitigating them?
  • How do we ensure AI decisions align with our values and ethics?
  • What backup plans exist if AI systems fail or underperform?
  • How are we monitoring AI bias and ensuring fairness?

Compliance and Legal:

  • How do we stay current with evolving AI regulations?
  • What legal and liability risks does AI create for our organization?
  • How do we ensure AI compliance across global operations?
  • What documentation and audit trails do we maintain for AI systems?
