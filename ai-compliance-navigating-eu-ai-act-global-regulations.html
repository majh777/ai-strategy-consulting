EU AI Act - Risk Categories

Prohibited AI: Social scoring, real-time biometric identification in public spaces
High-Risk AI: Critical infrastructure, education, employment, law enforcement
Limited Risk AI: Chatbots, deepfakes (transparency requirements)
Minimal Risk AI: Most business applications (voluntary compliance)
