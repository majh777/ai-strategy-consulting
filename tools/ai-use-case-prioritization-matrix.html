❓ How to Use the Prioritization Matrix

📝 Step 1: Define Use Cases

  • Brainstorm potential AI applications
  • Be specific about the business problem
  • Include technical requirements
  • Consider stakeholder impact

📊 Step 2: Score Each Criteria

  • 1-2: Low/Poor - Minimal benefit or high difficulty
  • 3: Medium/Average - Moderate benefit and effort
  • 4-5: High/Excellent - Maximum impact or ease
  • Be realistic and consistent in scoring

🎯 Step 3: Analyze Results

  • Quick Wins: High impact, high feasibility - Start here
  • Strategic: High impact, low feasibility - Long-term goals
  • Fill-ins: Low impact, high feasibility - Gap fillers
  • Avoid: Low impact, low feasibility - Deprioritize

🚀 Step 4: Build Roadmap

  • Prioritize quick wins for immediate value
  • Plan strategic projects for future phases
  • Consider resource allocation and dependencies
  • Review and update regularly
