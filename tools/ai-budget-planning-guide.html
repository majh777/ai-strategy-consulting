📋 AI Budget Planning Guidelines

💰 Budget Allocation Best Practices

  • Allocate 50-60% to talent and team development
  • Reserve 20-30% for infrastructure and platforms
  • Include 10-15% contingency for unexpected costs
  • Plan for 3-5 year investment cycles
  • Balance CapEx vs OpEx based on tax strategy

📊 ROI Measurement Framework

  • Track both hard and soft benefits
  • Measure time-to-value and implementation efficiency
  • Include productivity gains and cost avoidance
  • Monitor ongoing operational efficiency
  • Calculate net present value over 3-5 years

⚠️ Common Budget Pitfalls

  • Underestimating data preparation costs
  • Ignoring ongoing maintenance and operations
  • Insufficient allocation for change management
  • Not planning for model retraining costs
  • Overlooking compliance and governance needs

🎯 Optimization Strategies

  • Start with pilot projects to validate assumptions
  • Leverage cloud auto-scaling to optimize costs
  • Consider hybrid on-premise/cloud strategies
  • Invest in reusable platforms and frameworks
  • Build internal capabilities to reduce consulting
