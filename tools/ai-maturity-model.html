Reactive AI Maturity

Organizations respond to AI trends without systematic approach

📋 Key Characteristics

🎯 Strategy
No formal AI strategy; reactive to market pressure
👥 Team
Limited AI awareness; no dedicated expertise
🗄️ Data
Fragmented data with quality issues
🏗️ Technology
Legacy systems; minimal automation
📊 Governance
No AI governance framework
💰 Investment
Minimal, opportunistic AI spending

🚀 Path to Level 2

  • Conduct AI awareness training for leadership team
  • Identify 2-3 high-impact AI use cases
  • Assess current data quality and accessibility
  • Develop basic AI governance principles
  • Allocate budget for AI exploration
  • Hire or train initial AI talent

🔑 Key Enablers

Executive Sponsorship AI Education Data Audit Use Case Identification Budget Allocation

⏱️ Typical Timeline

6-12 months to establish foundation and move to Exploring level
