📋 RFP Question Framework

🏗️ Technical Questions

  • What AI/ML algorithms and frameworks does your platform support?
  • How do you handle model versioning and deployment?
  • What are your API rate limits and SLA guarantees?
  • How do you ensure model accuracy and prevent drift?
  • What integration options do you provide for existing systems?

🔐 Security & Compliance

  • What security certifications and compliance standards do you meet?
  • How is customer data protected and isolated?
  • What data residency and sovereignty options do you offer?
  • How do you handle data deletion and right to be forgotten?
  • What audit capabilities and logs do you provide?

🤝 Support & Services

  • What professional services do you offer for implementation?
  • What training and onboarding programs are available?
  • What are your support response times and escalation procedures?
  • How do you handle feature requests and product feedback?
  • What success metrics and reporting do you provide?

💰 Commercial Terms

  • What is your pricing model and how does it scale?
  • What are the total implementation and ongoing costs?
  • What payment terms and contract flexibility do you offer?
  • How do you handle scope changes and additional requirements?
  • What guarantees or penalties do you provide for SLA breaches?

🎯 Vendor Selection Decision Framework

1

Define Requirements

  • Document functional requirements
  • Set non-functional criteria
  • Define success metrics
  • Establish budget constraints
2

Market Research

  • Identify potential vendors
  • Review analyst reports
  • Gather initial information
  • Create vendor shortlist
3

Detailed Evaluation

  • Issue RFP to shortlisted vendors
  • Conduct demos and POCs
  • Score using evaluation matrix
  • Check references and case studies
4

Final Selection

  • Analyze evaluation results
  • Negotiate terms and contracts
  • Make final decision
  • Plan implementation approach

💡 Vendor Evaluation Best Practices

🎯 Clear Requirements

  • Define specific, measurable requirements
  • Prioritize must-have vs. nice-to-have features
  • Include non-functional requirements (performance, security)
  • Set clear evaluation criteria upfront

🔍 Thorough Due Diligence

  • Conduct proof of concepts with your data
  • Check vendor financial stability
  • Verify customer references and case studies
  • Review security and compliance documentation

🤝 Stakeholder Involvement

  • Include technical and business stakeholders
  • Get end-user input and feedback
  • Involve procurement and legal teams early
  • Ensure executive sponsorship and buy-in

📊 Objective Scoring

  • Use weighted scoring matrices
  • Document evaluation rationale
  • Consider total cost of ownership
  • Factor in long-term strategic fit
