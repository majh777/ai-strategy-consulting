When Big Four Makes Sense

To be fair, there are situations where Big Four firms might be appropriate:

  • Massive Scale: Projects requiring 50+ consultants across multiple countries and practices
  • Risk Aversion: Organizations that prioritize perceived safety over actual results

However, for most AI initiatives focused on practical results rather than political considerations, specialized expertise delivers superior outcomes.
