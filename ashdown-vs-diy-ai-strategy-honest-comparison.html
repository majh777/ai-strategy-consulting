When DIY Makes Sense

There are legitimate scenarios where developing AI strategy in-house might be appropriate:

  • Existing AI Expertise: You already have senior AI strategists on staff
  • Highly Unique Industry: Your industry has no comparable AI implementations
  • Extreme Security Requirements: Regulatory constraints prevent external consulting
  • Academic/Research Focus: Strategy development is part of research objectives
  • Unlimited Timeline: No urgency for AI implementation or competitive pressure

Reality Check: Less than 10% of organizations meet these criteria. Most benefit significantly from expert guidance.
